Tactile Ecology

Sound Visualization + Ecological Collaboration


Tactile Ecology is two-week interactive experience in which participants curate sounds, shape plants using sound patterns, care for plants, document care via an online garden, and interact with plants as sound producers. Tactile Ecology provides a multi-sensory experience incorporating visual, auditory, and tactile interactions to facilitate rich engagement between humans and plants.

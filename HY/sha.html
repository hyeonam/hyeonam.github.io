Shadow Friend

2008 Oct, Single Channel Video

Still Image


Video clip


Shadow Friend is a single channel video about imaginary friend next to me. In the solitary modern society, I made an imaginary friend not to feel alone. With dance waltz with my shadow, which mimics my behavior, I enjoyed the moment with imaginary friend hanging in the wall. Ridiculously what I was sticking in the wall and immersed in the moment makes me amused and escape from ordinary life routine.

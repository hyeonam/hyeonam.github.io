  • In-Between(Fake-Real)

    Detailed Picture (by Chang Kyun Kim)





  • In-Between(Fake-Real) is a site specific work in the window of Massage shop, Brooklyn. Through the window, people could appreciate the fake view in the real outside view. Also there are several LEDs turning on at night with light sensor like stars. I hope people could find the unexpected sight in the window and enjoy the real and fake scenes in the same view.
